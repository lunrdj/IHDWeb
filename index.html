About Me

Patti Toole is an interior designer, general contractor, and visionary with projects featured in publications like Denver Life Magazine.

For many, embarking on a home renovation project can be both exciting and overwhelming! You may have gathered some ideas but bringing those thoughts and ideas into reality is not easy to do without an experienced partner to guide you along the way. Intentional Home Designs works collaboratively with you to create beautiful, functional, and intentional spaces.

Founder, Patti Toole brings her passion for thoughtful design, her experience in residential kitchen and bath remodeling and her project management skills to every project.
