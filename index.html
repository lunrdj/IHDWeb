About Me

Patti Toole is an interior designer, general contractor, and visionary with projects featured in publications like Denver Life Magazine.

For many, embarking on a home renovation project can be both exciting and overwhelming! You may have gathered some ideas but bringing those thoughts and ideas into reality is not easy to do without an experienced partner to guide you along the way. Intentional Home Designs works collaboratively with you to create beautiful, functional, and intentional spaces.

Founder, Patti Toole brings her passion for thoughtful design, her experience in residential kitchen and bath remodeling and her project management skills to every project.

Services

My passion and goal is to help you make your interior design needs easy, efficient, and cost effective.

Concept

The best designs need to start somewhere! Our first step is to meet and discuss what you are looking for. In this step, we will develop a "moodboard" for your project. We will search for images, colors, or styles that you think will look amazing in your space.

Planning

I will work with you to plan a timeline for your project. In this step, we work together to measure out the space and develop a plan of action for the project.

Build!

It's finally time. At this step, I will bring in my team and get to work. Sit back, relax, and we will finish out the build in a timely manner, according to your schedule!
